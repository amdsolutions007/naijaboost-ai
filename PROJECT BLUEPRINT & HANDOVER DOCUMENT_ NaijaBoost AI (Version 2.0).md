### **3.0 Core Features & User Experience**

NaijaBoost AI is designed as a user's personal AI Social Media Manager, centered around a conversational, goal-oriented interface.

* **3.1 Conversational Interface:**  
  * **Multilingual Support:** The app's first prompt is a language selection: **English, Pidgin, Yoruba, Hausa, and Igbo**. The entire user experience is localized based on this choice.  
  * **Voice-First Accessibility:** Full voice-to-text and text-to-speech capabilities allow users to have a natural conversation with the AI, making it accessible to everyone, regardless of typing proficiency.  
* **3.2 Creative Suite (Brand Building):**  
  * **AI Idea Lab:** Acts as a business consultant, helping users brainstorm viable business ideas based on their interests and budget.  
  * **AI Brand Architect:** Generates business names, slogans, and professional logos using advanced image generation models.  
  * **AI Content Studio:** Assists in creating social media post captions, video scripts, and designs for promotional flyers.  
* **3.3 SMM Engine (Brand Growth):**  
  * **Goal-Oriented Campaigns:** Users state their goal (e.g., "make my song trend"), and the AI automatically designs a multi-platform, multi-service campaign.  
  * **Automated Execution:** The AI intelligently selects and orders services from the best-suited Tier 1 (Quality) or Tier 2 (Volume) API partners in the backend.  
  * **Unified Dashboard:** A simple, visual dashboard shows real-time campaign progress across all platforms.  
* **3.4 AI Analytics & Insights (Strategic Value):**  
  * Upon campaign completion, the AI delivers a simple performance report in plain language, providing actionable insights and recommendations for the user's next steps.
